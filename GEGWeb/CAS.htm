FINANCIAL AND TECHNOLOGY ADVISORY SERVICES

We are based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, with offices in the United Kingdom and India.

We work with premier financial institutions in key financial centers around the world, primarily in Beijing, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Singapore, UAE, Moscow, London and New York, working with a number of Banks, Financial Institutions and Export Credit Agencies (ECAs).

Our clients are established business corporations and government and quasi – government institutions and we strive to meet their diverse requirements.

We specialize in providing value added advice and services to our clients on complex strategic financial decisions and transactions, including technology transfers and related advisory and structuring services.

 Our scope of services include Working Capital Requirements, Equity & Debt Raising, Private Equity Arrangement, Joint Ventures and Mergers & Acquisitions.
